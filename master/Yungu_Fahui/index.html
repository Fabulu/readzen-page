Yungu Fahui 雲谷法會

School: Linji | 1500-1579 | Teacher: Wuwen Mingcong | Students: Hanshan Deqing

Ming dynasty Chan master who pioneered 7-day meditation retreats. Teacher of Hanshan Deqing, one of the Four Great Monks of the Ming.
