Muchen Daomin 木陳道忞

School: Linji | 1596-1674 | Teacher: Miyun Yuanwu | Students: Kuangyuan Benguo

First to succeed Miyun at Tiantong (1642). Summoned to Beijing as Chan teacher to the Shunzhi Emperor. CBETA: J26nB180, J26nB181.
