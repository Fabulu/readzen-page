Xutang Zhiyu 虛堂智愚

School: Linji | 1185-1269 | Teacher: Yunan Puyan

Late Song Linji master whose recorded sayings became profoundly influential in the later Linji tradition. Dharma heir of Yunan Puyan (not Wuzhun Shifan as sometimes misattributed).
