Xueqiao Yuanxin 雪嶠圓信

School: Linji | 1571-1647 | Teacher: Huanyou Zhengchuan

Third dharma brother of Miyun and Tianyin. Controversially claimed transmission from the long-dead Yunmen Wenyan.
