Fushan Fayuan 浮山法遠

School: Linji | 991-1067 | Teacher: Shexian Guisheng | Students: Touzi Yiqing

Received Linji transmission from Shexian Guisheng. Accepted Dayang Jingxuan’s Caodong materials and passed them to Touzi Yiqing, saving the Caodong school from extinction.
