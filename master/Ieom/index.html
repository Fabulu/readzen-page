Ieom 利嚴

School: Korean Seon | 869-936 | Teacher: Yunju Daoying

Late Silla / early Goryeo monk who studied under Yunju Daoying (Dongshan Liangjie lineage) in Tang China. Founded the Sumi-san school, the last of the Nine Mountain Schools. Attested primarily in Korean stele inscriptions.
