Lumen Zijue 鹿門自覺

School: Caodong | 1060-1117 | Teacher: Furong Daokai | Students: Puzhao Yibian

Direct disciple of Furong Daokai. His line carried Caodong transmission northward into Jin territory, eventually reaching Wansong Xingxiu via four generations — a separate branch from the southern Danxia Zichun line.
