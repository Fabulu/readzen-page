Hoam Chejeong 虎巖體淨

School: Korean Seon | Teacher: Hwanseong Jian | Students: Cheongbong Geoan

Obscure transmission link in the Pyeonyang lineage between Hwanseong Jian (d.1729) and Cheongbong Geoan (1710-1793). No independent biographical sources survive. Known only from Joseon-era lineage charts. The 68th Korean patriarch in the Jogye reckoning.
