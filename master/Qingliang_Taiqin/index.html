Qingliang Taiqin 清涼泰欽

School: Fayan | 940-970 | Teacher: Fayan Wenyi

Dharma heir of Fayan Wenyi. Resided at Qingliang monastery in Jinling. Famous for the tiger bell koan. Dates corrected: previously d.938 predates teacher Fayan (d.958).
