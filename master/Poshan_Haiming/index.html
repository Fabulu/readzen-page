Poshan Haiming 破山海明

School: Linji | 1597-1666 | Teacher: Miyun Yuanwu

Revived Chan in southwest China (Sichuan). Called 'Little Shakyamuni.' Founded Shuanggui Temple (1653). Had 87 dharma heirs.
