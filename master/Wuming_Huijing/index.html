Wuming Huijing 無明慧經

School: Caodong | 1548-1618 | Teacher: Yunkong Changzhong | Students: Wuyi Yuanlai, Yongjue Yuanxian

68th Caodong patriarch. Founded the Shouchang sublineage. Revived work-ethic monastic practice. Promoted Dahui's kanhua Chan within Caodong.
