Yinyuan Longqi 隱元隆琦

School: Linji | 1592-1673 | Teacher: Feiyin Tongrong

Chinese Chan master who emigrated overseas in 1654. Entirely Chinese-trained; his departure was a loss for Chinese Chan. CBETA: J27nB193.
