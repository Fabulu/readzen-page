Po'an Zuxian 破庵祖先

School: Linji | 1136-1211 | Teacher: Songyuan Chongyue | Students: Wuzhun Shifan

Dharma heir of Songyuan Chongyue. Teacher of Wuzhun Shifan, through whom his lineage produced Xueyan Zuqin and Gaofeng Yuanmiao.
