Huangbo Xiyun 黃檗

School: Hongzhou | 770-850 | Teacher: Baizhang Huaihai | Students: Linji Yixuan, Muzhou Daoming

Teacher of Linji and one of the most important Tang Chan masters. Born in Fuzhou, Fujian. Teaching centered on 'one mind' (一心): all beings possess this one mind which is the Buddha. His teachings recorded by lay student Pei Xiu in the Chuanxin Fayao (傳心法要) and Wanling Lu (宛陵錄). Named thirteen successors.
