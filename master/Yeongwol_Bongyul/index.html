Yeongwol Bongyul 永月奉律

School: Korean Seon | 1738-1823 | Teacher: Yongam Hyeeon | Students: Manhwa Boseon

Late 18th-early 19th century Seon master. The 73rd Korean patriarch. One of the few masters in the intermediate chain with attested dates (1738-1823). Teacher of Manhwa Boseon, who in turn taught Gyeongheo.

References: Terebess lineage
