Doyun 道允

School: Korean Seon | 797-868 | Teacher: Nanquan Puyuan

Silla monk who studied under Nanquan Puyuan in Tang China. Founded the Sajasan school, one of the Nine Mountain Schools of Korean Seon. His transmission from Nanquan places him in the Mazu Daoyi lineage.
