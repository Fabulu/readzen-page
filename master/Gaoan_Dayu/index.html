Gaoan Dayu 高安大愚

School: Hongzhou | 810-860 | Teacher: Guizong Zhichang | Students: Moshan Liaoran

Reclusive monk in Gao’an, Jiangxi. Triggered Linji Yixuan’s great awakening when Huangbo sent Linji to him. Teacher of Moshan Liaoran.
