Nanyuan Huiyong 南院慧顒

School: Linji | 860-930 | Teacher: Xinghua Cunjiang | Students: Fengxue Yanzhao

From Hebei. Taught at the South Hall (Nanyuan) of Baoying Monastery in Ruzhou with an extremely strict, confrontational style. Critical link in the Linji lineage: only through his line (via Fengxue Yanzhao) did Linji's transmission continue.

References: Terebess | BCR 38 (Hotetsu)
