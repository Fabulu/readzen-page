Jingqing Daofu 鏡清道怤

School: Chan | 868-937 | Teacher: Xuefeng Yicun

Heir of Xuefeng Yicun with five dharma successors. Best known for the 'voice of the raindrops' exchange, preserved as Book of Serenity case 46. Also appears in Blue Cliff Record cases 16, 23, and 46.
