Kumarajiva 鳩摩羅什

School: Early Chan | 344-413 | Teacher: Bandhudatta | Students: Sengzhao, Daosheng, Sengrui, Daorong

Directed one of the most prolific translation bureaus in Buddhist history. His translations shaped the vocabulary of East Asian Buddhism.
