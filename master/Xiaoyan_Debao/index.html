Xiaoyan Debao 笑巖德寶

School: Linji | 1512-1581 | Teacher: Wuwen Mingcong | Students: Huanyou Zhengchuan, Yunqi Zhuhong

Ming dynasty Linji master. Teacher of Yunqi Zhuhong. His teaching featured the stick and thunderous shout.
