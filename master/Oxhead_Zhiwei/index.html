Oxhead Zhiwei 牛頭智威

School: Niutou | 646-722 | Teacher: Niutou Fachi | Students: Helin Xuansu

Fifth patriarch of the Niutou (Oxhead) school. He maintained the community at Mount Niutou and transmitted the lineage to Helin Xuansu, the sixth and final patriarch. He appears in the Jingde Chuandeng Lu (juan 4).
