Kuangyuan Benguo 曠圓本果

School: Linji | d. 1700 | Teacher: Muchen Daomin | Students: Nguyen Thieu

Based at Longshan Temple in Guangdong. Transmitted Miyun's Linji lineage to the Vietnamese monk Nguyen Thieu, who brought it to Cochinchina in 1665.
