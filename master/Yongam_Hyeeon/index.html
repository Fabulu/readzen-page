Yongam Hyeeon 龍岩慧彦

School: Korean Seon | Teacher: Geumheo Beopcheom | Students: Yeongwol Bongyul

Transmission link in the Pyeonyang lineage. The 72nd Korean patriarch. According to Han Yongun's 'Brief Chronology,' Gyeongheo 'personally received the dharma inheritance from Yongam Seunim,' suggesting Yongam was still alive when Gyeongheo began his training, though the direct teacher-student relationship was with Manhwa Boseon.

References: Terebess lineage
