Hongcheok 洪陟

School: Korean Seon | 826-830 | Teacher: Xitang Zhizang

Early Silla monk who studied under Xitang Zhizang in Tang China and returned to establish the Silsangsan school, one of the Nine Mountain Schools of Korean Seon. His transmission is attested in Korean stele inscriptions and the Zutangji.
