Ningran Liaogai 凝然了改

School: Caodong | d. 1421 | Teacher: Songting Ziyan | Students: Jukong Qibin

Sixth-generation Shaolin Caodong master, heir of Songting Ziyan. Served as abbot of Shaolin Monastery in the early Ming, maintaining the Caodong transmission through the Yuan-Ming dynastic transition.
