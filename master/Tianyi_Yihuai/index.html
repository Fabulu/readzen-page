Tianyi Yihuai 天衣義懷

School: Yunmen | 993-1064 | Teacher: Xuedou Chongxian | Students: Huilin Zongben

One of Xuedou Chongxian's most important dharma heirs. He taught at Tianyi Monastery in Zhejiang and continued Xuedou's literary-poetic Chan. Through his student Huilin Zongben, who became abbot of the imperially sponsored Xiangguo Monastery in Kaifeng, his branch maintained a significant Yunmen institutional presence into the late 11th century.
