Jukong Qibin 俱空契斌

School: Caodong | d. 1452 | Teacher: Ningran Liaogai | Students: Wufang Kecong

Seventh-generation Shaolin Caodong master, heir of Ningran Liaogai. Served as abbot of Shaolin Monastery in the early-to-mid Ming dynasty, continuing the unbroken Caodong transmission established by Xueting Fuyu.
