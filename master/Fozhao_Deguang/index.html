Fozhao Deguang 佛照德光

School: Linji | 1121-1203 | Teacher: Dahui Zonggao

One of Dahui Zonggao's most prominent dharma heirs. Served as abbot at major Southern Song monasteries including Ayuwang (Ashoka) Temple and Lingyin Temple in Hangzhou. He also transmitted Dahui's kanhua Chan (keyword meditation) method to the next generation.
