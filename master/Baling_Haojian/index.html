Baling Haojian 巴陵顥鑒

School: Yunmen | 910-970 | Teacher: Yunmen Wenyan

Direct heir of Yunmen Wenyan, taught at Xinkai Temple in Baling (modern Yueyang, Hunan). Instead of written transmission documents, he used three turning phrases to convey the essence of the Yunmen school. Appears in Blue Cliff Record cases 13 and 100.
