Puzhao Yibian 普照一辨

School: Caodong | 1081-1149 | Teacher: Lumen Zijue | Students: Lingyan Sengbao

Dharma heir of Lumen Zijue. Continued Furong Daokai's Caodong line in the Jin-controlled north while the southern branch (Danxia Zichun to Hongzhi) flourished under the Song.
