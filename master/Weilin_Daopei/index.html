Weilin Daopei 為霖道霈

School: Caodong | 1615-1702 | Teacher: Yongjue Yuanxian

Only dharma heir of Yongjue Yuanxian. Established Gushan Yongquan Monastery as a hereditary Caodong dharma-transmission seat. The Gushan Caodong line continued through his successors for 250 years, eventually producing Xuyun.

References: Baidu | Terebess (Gushan lineage) | Western Chan Fellowship
