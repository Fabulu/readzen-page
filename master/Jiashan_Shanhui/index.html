Jiashan Shanhui 夾山善會

School: Chan | 805-881 | Teacher: Chuanzi Decheng

Originally a sutra lecturer in Guangzhou; redirected to study with the boatman-monk Chuanzi Decheng, who pushed him into the river, triggering his awakening. He then established a major monastery on Mount Jia (Jiashan) in Hunan, where he taught for decades. His heir Luopu Yuanan carried his transmission forward.
