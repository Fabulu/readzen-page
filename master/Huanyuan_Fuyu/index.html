Huanyuan Fuyu 還源福遇

School: Caodong | d. 1313 | Teacher: Lingyin Wentai | Students: Chunzuo Wencai

Third-generation Shaolin Caodong master (after Xueting Fuyu and Lingyin Wentai). Served as abbot of Shaolin Monastery during the late Yuan dynasty, maintaining the Caodong transmission that Xueting Fuyu had restored there.
