Xiaoshan Zongshu 小山宗書

School: Caodong | 1500-1567 | Teacher: Yuezhou Wenzai | Students: Yunkong Changzhong, Huanxiu Changrun

Shaolin Monastery abbot (1557-1566). Sent warrior monks against Japanese pirates. Key bridge figure connecting Yuan-era Shaolin Caodong to the late Ming revival.
