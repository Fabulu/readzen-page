Niutou Farong 牛頭法融

School: Niutou | 594-657 | Teacher: Daoxin | Students: Niutou Zhiyan

Founder of the Niutou (Oxhead) school. Studied under the Fourth Patriarch Daoxin but developed a distinct teaching emphasizing emptiness.
