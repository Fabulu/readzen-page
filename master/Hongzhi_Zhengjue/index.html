Hongzhi Zhengjue 宏智

School: Caodong | 1091-1157 | Teacher: Danxia Zichun | Students: Zide Huihui

Leading Caodong master. Advocate of silent illumination (mozhao) Chan. Compiled the Book of Equanimity.
