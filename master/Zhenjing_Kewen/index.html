Zhenjing Kewen 真淨克文

School: Linji | 1025-1102 | Teacher: Huanglong Huinan | Students: Doushuai Congyue

Also known as Baofeng Kewen. Second-generation Huanglong master. Known for expelling monks immediately after their awakening. Teacher of Doushuai Congyue.
