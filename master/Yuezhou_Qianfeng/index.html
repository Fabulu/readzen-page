Yuezhou Qianfeng 越州千峰

School: Caodong | 831-895 | Teacher: Dongshan Liangjie

Dharma heir of Dongshan. Known for 'three diseases and two lights of the dharmakaya' teaching. Yunmen served under him before succeeding Xuefeng. Appears in Gateless Gate Case 48 and Book of Serenity Case 61.

References: BOS 40 (Hotetsu) | Mumonkan Case 48
