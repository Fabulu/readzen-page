Foyin Liaoyuan 佛印了元

School: Yunmen | 1032-1098 | Teacher: Kaixian Shanxian

Northern Song Yunmen master who served as abbot at nine major monasteries including Jinshan and Lushan Kaixian. Emperor Shenzong bestowed on him a Goryeo robe, golden bowl, and the title 'Chan Master Foyin.' Close friend and teacher of the poet Su Shi (Su Dongpo), with whom he exchanged the famous 'Eight Winds' letter.
