Yulin Tongxiu 玉林通琇

School: Linji | 1614-1675 | Teacher: Tianyin Yuanxiu | Students: Maoxi Xingsen

Imperial Chan teacher to the Shunzhi Emperor. Titled Yulin Guoshi (National Teacher). Dissuaded the emperor from abdicating to become a monk.
