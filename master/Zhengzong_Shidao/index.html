Zhengzong Shidao 正宗實道

School: Linji | 1691-1753 | Teacher: Jialing Xingyin

Jialing Xingyin's dharma heir. 35th generation Linji (Yangqi branch). After him, the imperial Chan line faded — Qianlong shifted patronage to Tibetan Buddhism. Attested in DILA Authority Database (A026661).
