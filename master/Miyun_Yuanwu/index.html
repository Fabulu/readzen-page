Miyun Yuanwu 密雲圓悟

School: Linji | 1567-1642 | Teacher: Huanyou Zhengchuan | Students: Hanyue Fazang, Shiche Tongcheng, Feiyin Tongrong, Muchen Daomin, Poshan Haiming
