Juelang Daosheng 覺浪道盛

School: Caodong | 1592-1659 | Teacher: Wuyi Yuanlai

Founded the Caodong Tianjie lineage at Nanjing. Ming loyalist. Innovative synthesis of Chan and Confucian traditions.
