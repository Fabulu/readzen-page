Tianyin Yuanxiu 天隱圓修

School: Linji | 1575-1635 | Teacher: Huanyou Zhengchuan | Students: Yulin Tongxiu

Dharma brother of Miyun Yuanwu under Huanyou. Founded a separate Linji branch. Teacher of the imperial Chan line.
