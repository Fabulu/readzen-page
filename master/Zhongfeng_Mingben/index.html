Zhongfeng Mingben 中峰明本

School: Linji | 1263-1323 | Teacher: Gaofeng Yuanmiao | Students: Tianru Weize, Qianyan Yuanzhang

Most celebrated Yuan-dynasty master. Declined imperial appointments. Strong ties with Korean monks and extensive international connections.
