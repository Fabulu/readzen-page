Jialing Xingyin 迦陵性音

School: Linji | 1671-1726 | Teacher: Meng'an Chaoge | Students: Yongzheng Emperor, Zhengzong Shidao

Chan teacher of the Yongzheng Emperor before his enthronement. Died on Mount Lu; the emperor issued a special edict honoring his death.
