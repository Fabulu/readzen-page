Fayan Wenyi 法眼

School: Fayan | 885-958 | Teacher: Luohan Guichen | Students: Qingliang Taiqin, Tiantai Deshao, Baoen Xuanze

Founder of the Fayan school, last of the Five Houses. Emphasized integration of doctrinal study with Chan.
